Easy Collaboration

Create your project and share research PIN with your research team members.

Once you create an account, you will be able to create a research project by clicking middle button. If you already have a reseach PIN, you can sing-In either Analysit or Interventionist depend on PIN provided to you. Don't forget, for a specific project you can only take one role.
CREATING NEW PROJECT
App allows up to 7 participants study; however there is no limitation for researh-team. You can add as much as Interventionist and analysit you want. In this screen, research criterias must be specfied.
Once a project is created, you will receive two uniq reseach codes for each team. Don't forget; a reseacher can be assigned only one role in the project.
You will be able to navigate all your assigned or created research projects under two different tabs;Analyst and Interventionist.
To collect data; select the project after sing-in as Intervetionist,then click the particpant you want to add new data.
Analyst[s] will be able see time series graphs with real time update.
Once Intervetionist[s] finish collecting the data points, specified at the begining for each phase, analyst[s] will be able to access to decision screen.
If analysts request more data, they need to provide berief explanation.
In case there is a tie, analyst will receive new screen that shows other analysts decisions and comments.
Before making new decision, analyst[s] will be able discuss among themselves by using the post wall.
After all data collection process is done, analyst[s] will recieve final decision screen. They will guess what participant is introduced the intervention in which order.
